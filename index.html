About the Vision

A space conceived for unhurried evenings: quartz stone bar, humidor cabinets acting as room dividers, and attentive hosts who guide guests through rare vitolas. Every detail—from glassware to the Zuriga espresso ritual—is curated for calm.

The Lounge Experience

Quartz bar surfaces anchor the room, with vintage brass foot rails and discreet underlighting for a soft glow.

Lounge chairs in rich leather pair with low tables, framed by floor-to-ceiling humidor cabinets that define intimate corners.

Warm light cascades through linen shades while a curated analog audio setup provides gentle vinyl textures.

A Zuriga espresso corner and small-batch tea service offer refined pairings for every draw.
